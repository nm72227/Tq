The Corporate Heritage

Crafting an Authentic Culinary Revolution

TOUQAS FOODS arose out of a distinct vision to re-engineer fast comfort food dining. We realized that premium street food, local delicacies, and modern café culinary models could seamlessly combine if built upon an uncompromising base of hyper-fresh raw ingredients and operational discipline.

Our kitchens reject standard assembly-line corners. Every batch of momos is rolled by hand with calibrated wrap density; every burger patty is fresh, never chemically frozen; and our proprietary injection systems distribute deep-marinated flavors throughout the core muscle fibers of our broasts. We create experiences that respect the palette, cultivating true nutritional connection under our lifestyle guiding principle: “good food, great living”.

Core Corporate Mission

To deliver uncompromising, scientifically hygienic, high-vibrancy food products that blend gourmet excellence with structural affordability, transforming every singular meal into a memorable sensory connection.

Quality Assurance Mandate

Enforcing total structural trace validation across raw material inputs, cold chain infrastructure compliance, and state-of-the-art kitchen safety parameters to achieve perfect consistency.
